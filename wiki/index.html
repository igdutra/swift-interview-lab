Algorithms & Data Structures · Language: Swift · Theory masterfiles ↔ LeetCode walkthroughs

A personal reference wiki built as a linked pair of page types: theory masterfiles — timeless, Wikipedia-style references for one technique — and LeetCode walkthroughs — per-problem deep dives that instantiate a theory, including a full interview simulation. Every theory page links to the walkthroughs that demonstrate it; every walkthrough links back to its parent theory.

How to read it: start in a theory page to load the pattern, follow a blue cross-reference card to see it applied to a real problem, then follow the purple back-reference card to generalize again. Blue cards mean "go practice"; purple cards mean "go generalize".
