Isolation & Data Races

← Concurrency Theory · iOS Topic Hub · Actors · Sendable

How Swift eliminates data races by construction rather than by convention. Actors serialize access to the state they own; Sendable governs which values may cross between them. Together they are the two halves of compile-time concurrency safety.

The non-obvious hazard lives here too: actors are reentrant, so mutual exclusion protects each access without making a whole method atomic.
