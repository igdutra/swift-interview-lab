SwiftUI Fundamentals

iOS Topic Hub · Identity · State · Layout · Navigation

SwiftUI is declarative: you describe what the UI is for a given state, and the framework decides what to change on screen. Most SwiftUI bugs are not rendering bugs — they are identity bugs (the framework thought two views were the same, or thought they were different) or ownership bugs (state lives in the wrong place, so it resets or fails to propagate). The pages below take those concerns one at a time.
