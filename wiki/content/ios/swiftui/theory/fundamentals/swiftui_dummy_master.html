This page exists to demonstrate the ios-topic page type end to end: its section skeleton, its place in the generated table of contents, its hub card, and its position in the two-domain top navigation. Every section below states what belongs there rather than containing real material.

THE ONE IDEA: the ios-topic type differs from the LeetCode theory type in three ways — it has an API section (framework knowledge is signature-shaped), it splits Anti-Patterns out from Pitfalls (the source references are organized around wrong-then-right), and it drops complexity / solo, which are algorithm-only concerns.
