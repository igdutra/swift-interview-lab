Strings & Simulation

Theory Hub · Text Formatting · Simulation · String Parsing

String problems split along one axis: are you producing a string (or stepping a system) or consuming one? Producers walk an index and assemble characters or step a small state machine forward — digit-by-digit addition, a robot following moves, water settling into a terrain, words packed into justified lines. Consumers scan left to right and fold the characters into a structure — a number, a stack of operands, a nested list, a map of tokens. Almost every "implement / parse / simulate" prompt is one of these two, and naming which one you are looking at is the entire battle.
