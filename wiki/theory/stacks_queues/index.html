Stacks & Queues

Theory Hub · Stack (LIFO) · Queue (FIFO) · BFS · DFS · Parsing · Nesting

Two linear structures defined by their access discipline. A stack (LIFO) gives you the most recently added item first — nesting, bracket matching, expression evaluation, and iterative DFS all reduce to this mechanic. A queue (FIFO) gives you the oldest item first — level-order traversal, shortest-path BFS, and sliding time-windows all depend on it. The Overview is the decision layer with the Swift toolbox and cheat sheet for both; the deep dives below drill each structure against its canonical problems.
